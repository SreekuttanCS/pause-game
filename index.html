PAUSE THE TIME

How to play

In the Pause the Time game, your goal is to stop the timer exactly at the target time. You will be given two chances to match the target with the time you pause. If you succeed, you win the game! If not, you can try again until your two chances are over. Once the game ends, you can press the reset button to play again and improve your timing skills. Challenge yourself and see how accurate you can be!
